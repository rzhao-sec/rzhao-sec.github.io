Rui Zhao, ruizhao@unomaha.edu

Web Security and Privacy, System Security
Google Scholar   ORCID   DBLP
Rui Zhao received his B.E. degree in Information Countermeasures Technology and M.E. degree in Control Science and Engineering from the Xidian University, China, in 2004 and 2008, respectively, and his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the Colorado School of Mines (Mines) in 2016 under the supervision of Dr. Chuan Yue. He worked as a software engineer and a system engineer at ZTE Co. from 2008 to 2011 mainly on the design and development of the operation maintenance center, the element management system, and the operations support system.
